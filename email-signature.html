How to install it

  1. Click Copy signature above. It copies the formatted signature (not code) to your clipboard.
  2. Open your email client's signature settings and paste directly into the signature box.
  3. Set it as the default for new messages and replies. Send yourself a test to confirm the photo and links render.

Gmail

Settings → See all settings → General → Signature → Create new, then paste.

Apple Mail

Settings → Signatures → add one, uncheck "match default font," then paste.

Outlook

Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature, then paste.

One setup step: the photo loads from your live site at nicoleminoza.com/assets/signature-photo.png. Push assets/signature-photo.png to your repo first so the image resolves in everyone's inbox.
